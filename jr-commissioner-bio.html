Jr Commissioner aka "The Prodigy" Bio

DJ — The Jr. Commish

For two years, the league thought they were battling grown men.

They were wrong.

Operating under the alias DJ, this 10-year-old mastermind has been quietly pulling the strings behind his dad's team — drafting smarter, managing sharper, and stacking wins like it's light work. While others rely on gut feelings, DJ shows up with scouting reports, strategy, and a plan that most of you still don't understand.

Now, it's official.

He's no longer in the shadows.

DJ has been named the league's Jr. Commish.

Learning directly from the best, he's already proven he can outthink, outdraft, and outplay half the league — all before finishing his homework. No panic moves. No wasted picks. Just calculated domination.

And if this is what he looks like at 10?

Yeah... the future of this league might already be here.

Welcome to the era of the Jr. Commish.
